Hello!

I am currently a fifth year Ph.D. student studying mathematics at NYU Courant, intending to graduate spring 2025. My advisor is Eyal Lubetzky. I previously studied mathematics in my undergrad at UC Berkeley.

I am interested in probability theory, specifically lattice models in statistical mechanics. My work has been on interfaces in the low-temperature Ising and Potts models (and related models, e.g. random cluster, solid-on-solid).

Papers/Preprints

  • Logarithmic delocalization of low temperature 3D Ising and Potts interfaces above a hard floor with R. Gheissari and E. Lubetzky, submitted.
  • Critical wetting in the (2+1)D Solid-On-Solid model with R. Gheissari and E. Lubetzky, Electronic Communications in Probability, to appear.
  • Extrema of 3D Potts interfaces with E. Lubetzky, Communications in Mathematical Physics.
